About me

Welcome to my website! My name is Milo and I was a Semiconductor Engineer in Taiwan. Now I am a student at Humber College with a strong passion for technology and programming.

I am someone who loves to capture moments through the lens of a camera. Although photography isn't my major, it's a passion that has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. There's something about the way a single image can convey so much emotion and tell a story that has always fascinated me. Whenever I have a free moment, you'll find me with my camera in hand, exploring new places and capturing memories that will last a lifetime.
