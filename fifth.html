About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Milo and I was a Semiconductor Engineer in Taiwan. Now I am a student at Humber College with a strong passion for technology and programming.

I graduated from Union University and I major Electronic Engineering where I focused on semiconductor process development. Following my graduation, I joined Brooks Automation where I gained valuable experience in semiconductor machine maintenance and provided technical guidance to customers. During this time, I was exposed to software data and discovered a keen interest in programming. To further develop my skills, I decided to pursue additional education at Humber College.
